The Artist

Elena
Voss

Elena Voss is a Melbourne-based multidisciplinary artist whose practice spans painting, ceramics, textile, and photography. Her work moves between the monumental and the intimate — large-scale oil works hang alongside small ceramic assemblages, each in quiet conversation.


Trained at RMIT University and the Glasgow School of Art, Voss has exhibited across Australia, the UK, and Japan. Her work is held in private and institutional collections internationally, and she has received multiple Arts Victoria fellowships.
