About These Examples

The sites below are working prototypes built to showcase three distinct design approaches for artist portfolio websites. The artists — Elena Voss, Cora Ashby, and Sol Maren — are entirely fictional. All works shown are placeholder images. These examples are designed to give a clear sense of layout, typography, interaction style, and tone before any real content is applied. Every section you see — navigation, hero, gallery, collections, about, inspirations, social feed, and footer — would be populated with the real artist's work, biography, and brand.

Every Example Includes

Built-in Sections

01

Navigation

Fixed sticky nav with smooth scroll links, logo, and enquiry CTA. Glassmorphism on scroll.

02

Hero Section

Full-viewport entry — slider, static dramatic, or split screen. Each with animation and mobile fallback.

03

Artwork Gallery

Interactive gallery with hover reveals, title, medium, dimensions, and enquiry button per work.

04

About the Artist

Portrait, biography, stats (shows, collections, years), and downloadable CV link.

05

Collections

Works grouped by series. Visual grid or drag-scroll with titles, dates, and work counts.

06

Recent Works

Latest pieces with medium, year, and hover-reveal enquiry. Grid updates to feature new arrivals.

07

Inspirations

Artist statement, influences, source imagery. Gives collectors context for the practice.

08

Social + Footer

Instagram preview grid, newsletter subscription, navigation, legal, and social links.
